# Privacy Policy

**Version 1.0.0** | **Effective Date: January 26, 2026**

---

## Our Privacy Commitment

Sehat Locker is built on the principle that **your health data belongs to you**. Unlike cloud-based health apps, we've designed Sehat Locker to ensure your sensitive medical information never leaves your device without your explicit consent.

---

## 1. Data Collection & Storage

### Local-Only Storage
All your health data is stored **exclusively on your device**:
- Medical records and documents
- Doctor visit recordings and transcriptions
- Health metrics and observations
- Personal notes and reminders

### What We Do NOT Collect
- We do **not** upload your health data to any servers
- We do **not** collect personal identifiers
- We do **not** share data with third parties
- We do **not** use your data for advertising

---

## 2. Security Measures

### Military-Grade Encryption
Your data is protected with **AES-256 encryption**, the same standard used by governments and financial institutions worldwide.

### Biometric Protection
- Optional Face ID / Touch ID authentication
- PIN code backup for secure access
- Automatic session timeout for added security

### Local Processing
All AI analysis and document processing happens **entirely on your device**. No data is sent to external servers for processing.

---

## 3. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

### Access
View all your stored health information at any time.

### Export
Export your data in standard formats (PDF, JSON) for personal use or to share with healthcare providers.

### Deletion
Permanently delete any or all of your data at any time. Deleted data cannot be recovered.

### Portability
Take your health data with you. Export and transfer to other compatible applications.

---

## 4. Consent Management

### Granular Consent
You control which features have access to your data:
- Camera access for document scanning
- Microphone access for recording
- Calendar access for appointment reminders

### Revocation
Revoke any consent at any time through the app settings.

---

## 5. Data Retention

### Your Control
Data is stored on your device until you choose to delete it. We recommend regular backups to prevent data loss.

### Auto-Delete Options
Configure automatic deletion of:
- Recordings after processing
- Temporary files after export

---

## 6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

### Notification
You will be notified of any material changes through the app before they take effect.

### Continued Use
Your continued use of Sehat Locker after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

---

## 7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through the app's feedback feature.
